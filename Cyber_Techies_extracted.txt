===== PAGE 2 =====
Overview of your solution
• The Idea: A safe online platform that lets verified community members list and trade extra LPG cylinders 
when there are problems with supply.
• The goal is to connect people who have extra cylinders with people who are running low on them in a 
dangerous way, so that no one has to use unsafe fuel alternatives.
• Value Proposition: Offers a safe, structured, and verified alternative to the informal and risky "cylinder 
borrowing" that happens in neighborhoods right now.

===== PAGE 4 =====
Tech Stack Used 
• Frontend: React.js and Tailwind CSS make a mobile-first UI that works on all devices.
• Backend: Render.com hosts the Node.js Express server.
• Database and authentication: Firebase Firestore (NoSQL) and Firebase Phone Authentication.
• Maps and location: Leaflet.js with OpenStreetMap (an API that doesn't cost anything).
• Claude API (Anthropic) is the AI Engine for the "Safety Advisor" chatbot.

===== PAGE 5 =====
Real World Use Case 
• Scenario: A family runs out of gas while waiting for a delivery in a Mumbai suburb because of the monsoon.
• Use: They log into ShareGas, find a neighbor 500 meters away who has an extra cylinder, and make a 
verified exchange in less than 30 minutes.
• Safety Assurance: Before the transaction is marked as complete, the app makes both users do a "Leakage & 
Connection" safety check.
